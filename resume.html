Background

I am an aquatic ecologist currently studying how we can use Environmental DNA (eDNA) to detect Aquatic Invasive Species (AIS). Broadly my research interests involve how anthropogenic factors impact aquatic ecosystems and how we can mitigate these impacts. Currently I work for the Minnesota Aquatic Invasive Species Research Center (MAISRC) so most of my current research revolves around Aquatic Invasive Species.
